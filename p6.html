You “That’s not due for another 3 weeks, right? We have plenty of time. It’s Thursday, we can make a start on it, this weekend.”

Madison’s lips are pursed. You can see the hostility bubbling under the surface. As she insists on saying nothing, you say nothing, and you’re both fixed in a stalemate, until you break it “Good talk. I’ll see you tomorrow then.”
