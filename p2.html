I have a friend who considers me her BFF.

Madison: “Happy National Best Friends Day! Men, babies, doesn’t matter (no one else matters?)…. We. Are. SOULMATES!!"

The thing is, I don’t feel the same way.

You: “Heh, yeah, who needs them? You… too"

Madison: “I dropped a gift round your house first thing” “I hope you love it”

First thing? She must have got here at dawn. You head downstairs to find a small parcel outside, at the foot of the door.

You read the card. It says “…..”

You then open parcel, it’s a bracelet, you put it on. You start to think that maybe you’re being to harsh on her recently. She’s thoughtful, and she just really cares about you. Maybe you could make more of an effort.

You get ready and head out for school.
